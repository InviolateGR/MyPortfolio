ADMIN's HUB

Project Type

Project Overview

Admin'sHUB is a comprehensive HR/Employer Dashboard App designed to streamline and enhance the efficiency of human resource management within a company. The app provides HR professionals with a powerful platform to oversee and manage various aspects of their company's operations.

My Contribution

In the development of Admin'sHUB, I was responsible for the following contributions:

UI/UX Design:

Led the design process using Figma, creating a user-friendly and visually appealing interface for the entire app.

Feature Development:

Designed key features such as the Dashboard, Message Center, Task Keeper, and File Manager, ensuring they meet the needs of HR professionals.

Navigation Enhancement:

Implemented side menu bars to improve the app's navigation and user experience.

Page Creation:

Developed the landing page, login and signup pages, and the calendar feature.

Dark Mode Layout:

Created a complete dark mode layout to offer users a modern and eye-friendly alternative.

Detail Refinement:

Made numerous small adjustments and enhancements across the design to ensure consistency and usability.
